Privacy Policy

Welcome to JobzQuest! This Privacy Policy explains how JobzQuest collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information when you use our website. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information: When you create an account on JobzQuest, we may collect certain personal information, such as your name, email address, contact details, and optionally, your resume or CV. This information is necessary to create and manage your account, provide you with access to our services, and connect you with relevant job opportunities. 1.2 Usage Information: We automatically collect certain information when you visit or interact with our website. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and other usage data. We use this information to improve our services, analyze trends, and enhance the user experience.

We do not control how they handle your personal information, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information. 2.4 Aggregated Data: We may anonymize and aggregate user data to generate statistical insights, perform market research, and improve our services. This data does not personally identify you.
